# Additional and Improved Glossary Entries

## Job Status

The current stage of a vehicle repair or service process within the workshop workflow. Job statuses help workshop staff and customers track vehicle progress from booking to completion.

### Example Statuses

* Booked In
* Inspection Started
* Awaiting Quote Approval
* In Progress
* Awaiting Parts
* Ready for Collection
* Completed

---

## Service Advisor

A workshop staff member responsible for customer communication, job card management, quote coordination, and workflow updates throughout the repair process.

---

## Quality Check

The final inspection stage performed after repairs are completed to ensure work quality, safety, and customer satisfaction before vehicle collection.

---

## Workshop Dashboard

The main system interface used by workshop staff to manage vehicles, customers, job cards, quotes, invoices, notifications, and workflow updates.

---

## Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

A security approach where system users are granted access permissions based on their assigned roles within the workshop system.

### Example Roles

* Workshop Admin
* Service Advisor
* Mechanic
* Customer

---

## Status History

A chronological record of all job status changes linked to a vehicle job card, including timestamps and the user responsible for each update.

---

## Customer Tracking Page

A customer-facing page that allows customers to monitor vehicle repair progress, quote approvals, parts updates, and collection readiness without contacting the workshop directly.

---

## VIN (Vehicle Identification Number)

A unique alphanumeric code used to identify a specific vehicle for tracking, registration, and workshop management purposes.
