## User-story: ...

**As a** Returning client
**I want** to be able to log into an account 
**So that** my information is private and protected, and also to see my vehicle's information (Quotes, Balance and Progress)

## Acceptance Criteria:
    -  Clients can use their emails and passwords to log into their accounts.
    - Users that have accounts will be redirected to their dashboards where they can acces information like Quotes, Balances and Progress and users who don't have accounts yet will be given the option to create one.
    - Clients can't access each others information, only the users with access to that specific dashboard will have access to that clients information.
