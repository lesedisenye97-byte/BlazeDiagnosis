## User-story: ...

**As a** Receptionist
**I want** to be able to navigate through the data of customers to write quotes and invoices out to them in an organized manner and to give an estimated date of completion
**So that** the receptionist can complete their quotes faster and more effectively, and be able to process quotes and invoices

## Acceptance Criteria:
    -  The application can have a auto-sum function that calculates the estimated price of each service that the client asked for.
    - A quick search bar where the receptionist can look up clients by their name, number or license plate number should be implemented for faster navigation.
    - An estimated time calendar should be added that will be automatically updated to let the client know when their vehicle will be ready for collection based on the services added to the invoice/quote.
